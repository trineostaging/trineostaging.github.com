Trineo Bespoke Ruby on Rails Solution covered on ReadWriteWeb

As we respectively relaxed on Christmas eve, Abhinav received an email from a friend in Australia congratulating him on being covered on the very well known technology blog ReadWriteWeb. He quickly shared the news with Dan – we were both absolutely taken by surprise. Talk about a great Christmas present …

The ReadWriteWeb article described the rapid development of a prototype customer portal which Abhinav built. It was built using the Ruby on Rails framework running on the Heroku platform and it talked to a customer’s Salesforce org using the Salesforce API. Combining Daniel’s Salesforce mastery and Abhinav’s Rails skills, the portal was developed in a few days for presentation to the customer.

The article extended and recapped the writing of our colleague Ben Kepes.

We’re humbled and really excited to receive such coverage, and are fielding queries based on this write-up.
